White dragon tea (白龙花茶) is a tea made from the flowers and leaves of the white dragon bush, a rare flowering plant. The plant is known for producing a delicious tea, which has led to it being overharvested. It has since become a rarity, and, as such, has become highly prized by makers of fine teas.

The white dragon bush naturally inhabits the eastern Earth Kingdom and grows best in forested areas where there is an abundance of shade.

Due to its appearance, it is sometimes confused with the poisonous white jade bush. Ingesting the latter causes a severe reaction that can cause swelling of the throat and death. White dragon petals are reddish-pink with white streaks, while white jade petals are white with dark-orange streaks.
