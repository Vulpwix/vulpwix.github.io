Lychee tea (荔枝茶) is a black tea scented by the lychee nut and enjoyed for its strong and pleasantly sweet fruity aroma. The brewed tea naturally has a reddish-brown hue.

The lychee nut is a small, plum-sized red fruit with a rough outer skin and sweet juicy flesh. It grows on trees and is readily picked in mid-summer. Due to the Lychee nut's availability, it is commonly found in many parts of the globe. It is especially enjoyed by the citizens of the Earth Kingdom.
