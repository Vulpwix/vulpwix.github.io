Ginger tea (姜母茶) is a beverage made from the ginger root. It has a long history as a traditional medicine in Asian cultures.

Ginger tea is commonly prepared by boiling fresh ginger slices in water or mixing ginger juice with hot water. It is often served with honey, sugar, or other sweetener. In some cultures, spices and milk are also added.

Ginger hosts a variety of health benefits ranging from pain relief to cancer prevention.
