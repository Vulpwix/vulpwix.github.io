Green tea (绿茶) is a popular tea with various flavours and types and is the most widely consumed tea in the world. It is known for its light, earthy and sometimes grassy taste.

The history of green tea reaches back to almost 600 AD in China (Tang Dynasty). It has long been known for its health benefits.

Green tea is processed and grown in a variety of conditions, resulting in various types of green tea. Green tea takes an important place in Chinese, Korean, and Japanese cultures.
